The S Temperature in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to the S Temperature in 5/9 times (degrees Fahrenheit (°F) minus 32).

°C = 5/9 x (°F - 32).

The S temperature in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) is equal to the S Temperature in (9/5 times a degree Celsius (°C)) plus 32.

°F = (9/5 x °C) + 32.
